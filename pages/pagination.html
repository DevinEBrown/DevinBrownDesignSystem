Navbar

The navbar houses the design system'bbranding and allows users to easily navigate through key pages of the website.

Logo Placement:The website logo is placed at the top-left corner of the navigation bar for brand recognition and easy identification. The logo is a vector image that scales gracefully across different screen sizes.

Responsive Navigation:
On smaller screens (e.g., tablets and mobile devices), the navigation bar condenses into a hamburger menu icon.
Clicking the hamburger icon reveals a vertically stacked menu for easy navigation on small screens.
Each menu item is touch-friendly, allowing users to tap easily without accidentally activating other items.
